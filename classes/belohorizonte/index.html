Belo Horizonte, Brazil

Instructor: Sandra Arruda

Sandra Arruda

Sandra has practiced Tai Chi Chuan Yang Style since 2005 and in 2008 she had the opportunity of attend Master Alex Dong Workshop in Belo Horizonte, Brazil. In that occasion, the talent, power and softness of Master Alex, also his open and clear teachings, impressed her. Since then, Sandra has followed Master Alex in workshops in Brazil and USA.

Dong Style has changed totally her perspective about Tai Chi Chuan and her goals to improve herself, getting deep into her practice and share her learning. In 2010, she started teaching: an honor and an opportunity to contribute in the spread and growth of this art.
