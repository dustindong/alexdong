London, England

Classes in Yang style tai chi chuan (taijiquan) are held in several locations in London. Beginners always welcome; no previous experience necessary. Intermediate classes require suitable experience.

Your first class will be a gentle introduction to the tai chi movements, including a standard set of exercises to warm up the body and relax the mind ready for the class. We suggest that you wear soft shoes and loose clothing; something like a track suit and trainers. Most classes do not require you to book in advance and classes can be joined at any time.
