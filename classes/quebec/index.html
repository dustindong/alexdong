Philippe Daviet

Philippe has gravitated around various martial arts during a large part of his life. His interest in Taiji started in 2001 after having sustained injuries from his other martial activities. He has been attending Master Dong’s workshops since 2010.

Both the martial intent and the Taoist philosophy have an equal footing during his classes to respect Taijiquan’s origins.

Philippe began instruction in 2013 in the city of Magog, Canada.
