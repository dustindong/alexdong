Westwater

April 24th 2015 - May 2nd 2015

Westwater Intensive Training 2015

Friday April 24th to Saturday May 2nd 2015

Highlights will be to reteach the Fajin form and work on Fajin energy, Push Hands and weapons. More details to follow.

West Water camp is located on the beautiful high desert of the Utah - Colorado border, forty five minutes from the Grand Junction, Colorado Walker Field airport and five hours from Denver and Salt Lake City. It's warm during the day and cool at night. At 5000 feet in elevation, the stars and moon are more beautiful and bright. Miles away from civilization, for nearly a decade students from around the world have found the solitude, and nature of the area, a powerful adjunct to their study of Taj Ji.

This camp has no indoor sleeping arrangements; everyone has to bring their own tent and bedding for camping, and personal things like towel, soap, shampoo, sun block, hat and flip flops. Keep in mind late night temperature could drop down to freezing. Meals, showers, washing machine and two flushing toilets along with dining and training halls are provided.

VENUE DETAILS FOR THE PUSH HANDS WORKSHOP

The venue is called Ktima Maliveni and is a place designed especially for workshops situated outside of town on the hills. It is also an organic farm very close to Athens International Airport (15 minutes drive) near Markopoulo.

Rooms: Rooms have their own bathroom and can host comfortably up to 3 people with upper and lower space. There is also a space with 10 beds and shared bathrooms (this deducts 12€ from the total cost).

Practice Places: There is an outdoor shaded space for practice as well a big hall in case of bad weather.

Transportation: You can arrange you own taxi transportation from and to the airport, individually or for small group of people (3-4) who arrive approx the same time. Special details of how to find the place and taxi costs will be given once you book for the workshop.

Getaway: There is a small town by the sea - Porto Rafti - approx. 10 minutes drive from the venue. Several beaches are nearby if you want to swim. For those who like to try local food there are several fish restaurants.
